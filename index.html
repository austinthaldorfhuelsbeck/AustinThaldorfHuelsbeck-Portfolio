About

Hey!

My name is Austin, and I'm learning to be a web developer.
The past ten years have brought a variety of professional opportunities for me:

  • Barista
  • Video editor
  • Touring rock band member
  • Record producer
  • Night shift VHS ingest guy
  • And many more!

In fact, even before making my first latte in 2011, I was writing code for my high school's FIRST Robotics team.
Before that, I was learning Flash and Dreamweaver to create webpages and animations in middle school.
And before that, I was programming choose-your-own-adventure games on my TI-83+ graphing calculator.

The threads tying all these disparate experiences together are a love for computers and technology, a love for solving problems, and a love for working with people. Whether that means picking the perfect pedal for a guitar solo on stage, the perfect microphone and preamp for a vocalist's tone in the studio, or the perfect lens to bring out a groom's lapel pin on his wedding day, I have always been drawn towards opportunities to use technical knowledge to help create something awesome with other people.

This winding road of personal and professional experience has led me directly to where I am today: studying web development. I cannot wait for the opportunity to grow upon my curiosity, and to turn it into a rewarding career collaborating with others.
